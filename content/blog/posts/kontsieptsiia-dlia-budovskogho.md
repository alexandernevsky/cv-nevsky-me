и мышцами.</p><h3 id=\"2-%D1%84%D1%83%D0%BD%D0%BA%D1%86%D0%B8%D0%BE%D0%BD%D0%B0%D0%BB%D1%8C%D0%BD%D0%BE%D1%81%D1%82%D1%8C-%D0%BA%D0%B0%D0%BA-%D1%8F%D0%B4%D1%80%D0%BE\"><strong>2) Функциональность как ядро</strong></h3><p>Мы не «делаем зубы». id=\"3-%D1%8D%D1%81%D1%82%D0%B5%D1%82%D0%B8%D0%BA%D0%B0-%D0%BA%D0%B0%D0%BA-%D1%81%D0%BB%D0%B5%D0%B4%D1%81%D1%82%D0%B2%D0%B8%D0%B5-%D0%B3%D0%B0%D1%80%D0%BC%D0%BE%D0%BD%D0%B8%D0%B8\"><strong>3) Эстетика id=\"%D1%87%D0%B5%D0%BC-smile-renaissance-%D0%BE%D1%82%D0%BB%D0%B8%D1%87%D0%B0%D0%B5%D1%82%D1%81%D1%8F-%D0%BE%D1%82-%C2%AB%D0%BE%D0%B1%D1%8B%D1%87%D0%BD%D0%BE%D0%B9-%D1%8D%D1%81%D1%82%D0%B5%D1%82%D0%B8%D0%BA%D0%B8%C2%BB\"><strong>Чем
