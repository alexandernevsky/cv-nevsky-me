  # Website design for The Driven podcast

  _September 2025_  
  Web designer

  I designed the podcast website in Figma: I assembled a modular grid, a component system for episodes and guests, and drew card states and micro-animations. I thought through the typography and color so that the covers and previews were “readable” even in the feed. At the content level, there are uniform templates for issue, guest, and collections so that the team can quickly assemble new pages without scattering.

  ![](https://cdn.nevsky.me/content/images/2026/03/the-driven-web-site__01.webp)

  ### Website installation in Framer

  Then I laid out everything in Framer: I moved the repeating blocks into components with options, set up smooth transitions, and adaptation for mobile/tablet/desktop.

  Connected Framer CMS (content management system), built in players, mailing forms and basic technical optimization for search engines (search engine optimization): meta tags, Open Graph, site map. Pictures - with lazy-loading and preview generation so that the site opens quickly.

  **The result is a clean, fast and easily scalable platform for new episodes.**
