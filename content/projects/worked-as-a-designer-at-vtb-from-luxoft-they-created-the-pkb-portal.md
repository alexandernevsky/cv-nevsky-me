body_en: "# Worked as a designer at VTB from Luxoft - they created the PKB portal\n\n_January 2018 – September 2018_  \n**Senior Product Designer**\n\n\U0001FAE1 Worked at Luxoft **from January 2018 to September 2018** senior product designer\n\n> In 2018, VTB Bank is transferring the group's divisions to a single information platform, the Corporate Business Portal. The project provides for the introduction of tools for cross-functional interaction between group employees, a platform for the bank’s corporate business call center, and also involves the use of intelligent information processing algorithms.\n\n### The Wolf of Wall Street\n\nIn 2018, VTB Group transferred its divisions to a single platform **\"Corporate Business Portal\" (CBP)** : cross-functional interaction, call center workplace, smart data processing and unified scenarios for the front office.\n\nWe sat in **\"Federation Tower\"** in Moscow City: glass to the sky, badge-turnstiles, negotiations on the schedule. I came out of the elevator in a blue jacket, shirt and tie - a little “Wolf of Wall Street”, but in fact - this was the engineering of business processes and interfaces.\n\n### My role\n\nMy tasks as an outstaff designer from Luxoft included supporting the sections of the PKB “Clients”, “Reports” and “Sales”.\n\n### What did you do about the product?\n\nIn \"Clients\" we collected **consolidated 360° card** : transactions, interactions, documents, limits - all in one screen with quick actions and role-based visibility of fields.\n\n![](https://cdn.nevsky.me/content/images/2026/03/vtb-luxoft__01.webp)Consolidated card 360°\n\nIn \"Sales\" - **funnels and plan-fact** , statuses, reminders, visual risk markers. I thought about it separately **empty states** , skeleton loading and hints - so that the interface remains “alive” even with heavy data.\n\n![](https://cdn.nevsky.me/content/images/2026/03/vtb-luxoft__02.webp)\n\nIn “Reports” I designed **interactive dashboards** with smart filters, saved views and export.\n\n_A few more screens:_\n\n![](https://cdn.nevsky.me/content/images/2026/03/vtb-luxoft__03.png)\n\n### Bottom line\n\nPKB has become not just a “portal”, but **working workplace** for corporate business: faster access to data, fewer clicks in typical scenarios, a single visual language and predictability.\n\n_And for me this was an important stage - design at the level of a large financial machine, where the beauty of the interface is measured by the speed of the transaction and the calmness of the operator on the line._"
