  # Sell ​​One Video: turning future video income into money today

  _June - October 2025_  
  **Contract designer**

  > Creators often need cache “now” and AdSense pays “later”. Fundmates has packaged this into a simple product: you enter a link to the video, log in via Google, receive a forecast of income and a fixed offer with price analysis (forecast → risk → commission). Nobody takes away the rights to the video - only the income stream is ceded.

  I designed a disparate manual process into a single chat flow: monetization and Content ID verification, electronic signature, legal communication through the Multi-Channel Network (MCN), payment - and transaction status in your personal account. Instead of “seven steps and calls” - a clear path in minutes.

  In beta, the “time to offer” was reduced from days to minutes, and the conversion from interest to deal increased due to transparent mathematics. Next comes AI content scoring and packages of several videos.

  ### My role

  Product strategy, UX, prototypes and integration with the legal framework and integrations.
