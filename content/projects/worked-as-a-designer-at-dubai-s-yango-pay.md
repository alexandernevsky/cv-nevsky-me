access global payments without the need for a physical bank account.\n  * **Foundation of the credit vertical for drivers** : microloans for taxi drivers with a scaling plan **Buy Now, Pay Later** (payment in four installments) for a wide audience.\n  * **Updated mobile money streams** : improved UX of top-ups and P2P transfers, tailored to user habits and popular payment methods.\n\n![](https://cdn.nevsky.me/content/images/2026/03/working-with-yango-pay__03.jpg)Splash screen, home screen and card screen in Yango Pay app\n\nThe overall goal is to make financial interactions clear, inclusive and emotionally engaging for millions of people in emerging markets.\n\n### Yango Pay app screens\n\n![](https://cdn.nevsky.me/content/images/2026/03/working-with-yango-pay__04.png)\n\n### Emotional translations: money with human warmth\n\n> In Africa, a lot of transfers are still made through USSD/SMS codes - quickly, but “without face”.\n\nWe've added a lively layer of emotion to Yango Pay, reflecting the local spirit of joy and celebration: you can attach an emoji and a short message to your transfer.\n\nOn the recipient’s side, this is not just a notification - it’s a moment: attached emoji “sprinkle out” on the screen like confetti; with soft vibration and short sound. An ordinary transaction is transformed into a warm, memorable gesture between people.\n\n![](https://cdn.nevsky.me/content/images/2026/03/working-with-yango-pay__05.jpg)Emotional translations\n\nTechnically, everything is safe and respectful of traffic: the animations are light, with a weak Internet there is a simple fallback without visual effects, and for compliance there is filtering/moderation of attachments. The result is the same translation, but with human meaning.\n\n### AI postcards for translations: a personal picture in a couple of seconds\n\n> We “screwed” the generation of images directly into the flow of sending money: the sender selects a topic (for example, “payday”, “thank you”, “happy birthday”), sets a couple of words - and the AI ​​collects a mini-card in 1-2 seconds.\n\nIt can be edited (signature, emoji), and it flies away along with the translation. The recipient sees the money and immediately a personal picture - not a dry notification, but a small reason to smile.\n\n![](https://cdn.nevsky.me/content/images/2026/03/working-with-yango-pay__06.jpg)AI generation of images to insert into translation\n\nThe result is that an ordinary P2P translation turns into a “message with meaning”: quickly, beautifully and humanly, in the spirit of the local holiday culture.\n\n## Landing page Yango Pay\n\n> To grow the product and educate users, I designed a separate landing page for Yango Pay.\n\nIt briefly and clearly reveals key scenarios: replenishment through mobile money, P2P transfers, “emotional” payments, issuance of virtual cards.\n\n![](https://cdn.nevsky.me/content/images/2026/03/working-with-yango-pay__07.jpg)Home page of the Yango Pay landing page\n\nThe design is light, mobile-first and localized in visual language and tone. The layout is adaptive, scalable, and compatible with the Yango design system.\n\nWe tested different versions of the hero block and CTA to increase conversion. The page has become a working tool for onboarding, partner presentations and increasing confidence in the product.\n\n![](https://cdn.nevsky.me/content/images/2026/03/working-with-yango-pay__08.jpg)Landing page Yango Pay\n\n### Agent management web interface\n\nIn the Yango Pay ecosystem, I also designed a web panel for managers who oversee networks of exchange agents:\n\n  * structure and management of the list of agents (creating/editing/deleting profiles),\n  * real-time dashboard for key metrics: number of transactions, current balances, calculated commissions,\n  * activity and performance monitoring for network transparency and manageability.\n\n\n\nThe focus is clarity, speed and convenience: a complex operating system is reduced to a clear and fast control panel.\n\n![](https://cdn.nevsky.me/content/images/2026/03/working-with-yango-pay__09.webp)Main screen of the Yango Pay Agent website![](https://cdn.nevsky.me/content/images/2026/03/working-with-yango-pay__10.webp)Yango Pay Agent web application dashboard![](https://cdn.nevsky.me/content/images/2026/03/working-with-yango-pay__11.webp)List of agents in the Yango Pay Agent application\n\n### Gratitude\n\nProject **Yango Pay** is not just a line on a resume, but an opportunity to create technology that significantly helps people.
