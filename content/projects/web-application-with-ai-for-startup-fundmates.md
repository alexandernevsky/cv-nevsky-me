  # Web application with AI for startup Fundmates

  > As part of the work, I designed and designed the concept of an AI chat: an interface where the creator gives access to the analytics of his channel, receives an automated analysis from Fundmates and, based on it, personal offers for financing, in order to then select the best option directly in the dialogue

   _June - October 2025_  
  Contract designer

  ![](https://cdn.nevsky.me/content/images/2026/03/fundmates-ai-app-cv__01-1.webp)

  ### About the project

  One of my recent projects was developing an end-to-end conversational script for Fundmates, a US startup that allows YouTube creators to receive upfront funding directly through chat. Instead of complex forms or front-end panels, creators interact with the AI ​​assistant through a familiar messaging interface, making the experience seamless and human.

  ![](https://cdn.nevsky.me/content/images/2026/03/fundmates-ai-app-cv__02-1.webp)Main application screens

  ### My role

  I mapped and prototyped the entire user journey, from YouTube authentication to analytics, personalized funding offers, and agreements.

  The challenge was balancing transparency and trust (creators sharing sensitive data) with simplicity and speed (our goal is to complete the entire process in five minutes).

  ### Design process

  Working in the fast pace of design sprints, I created wireframes, UX copy for conversations, and high-fidelity prototypes that we tested with real creators in an iterative manner.

  I focused on the tone of communication, the clarity of every step, and the micro-interactions that make AI more accessible rather than transactional.
