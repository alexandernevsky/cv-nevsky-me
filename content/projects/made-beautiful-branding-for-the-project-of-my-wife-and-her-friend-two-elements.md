  # Made beautiful branding for the project of my wife and her friend Two Elements

  _June 2019 – July 2023_  
  Side project of wife and her friend

  ![](https://cdn.nevsky.me/content/images/2026/03/two-elements__01.png)

  ### Yulia and Irina are the authors of the project

  ![](https://cdn.nevsky.me/content/images/2026/03/two-elements__02.png)

  ### Sign and style

  In the center is a minimalist logo: two swans forming a heart. It's about balance, connection and warmth. The soft pastel palette, handwritten accents and clean packaging layouts give a sense of intimacy and a grown-up aesthetic. The brand becomes recognizable and warm at first sight.

  ![](https://cdn.nevsky.me/content/images/2026/03/two-elements__03.png)

  ### Premium option

   _Option of a premium collection or a collection for men with black wax._

  ![](https://cdn.nevsky.me/content/images/2026/03/two-elements__04.png)

  ### Package

  The identity is deployed throughout the entire system: candle jar, wooden (oak) lid, box. Everything speaks the same visual language - from the logo to the font - and turns the candle from a “product on the shelf” into a small ritual.

  ![](https://cdn.nevsky.me/content/images/2026/03/two-elements__05.png)![](https://cdn.nevsky.me/content/images/2026/03/two-elements__06.png)

  ### What it looks like in real life

  The result turned out great. The packaging feels dense and premium, and the oak lids with laser engraving are absolutely stunning: a detail that immediately elevates the perception of the product.

  ![](https://cdn.nevsky.me/content/images/2026/03/two-elements__07.png)

  In reality, the identity came out even better than on the mockups: soft colors and neat typography work equally well on both the box and the can, and the warm wood texture adds tactility—something you can’t feel in the picture.

  ![](https://cdn.nevsky.me/content/images/2026/03/two-elements__08.png)

  ### Bottom line

  The Two Elements identity isn't just about aesthetics—it's about authenticity and care. These candles feel like a thoughtful gift and a design object at the same time.

  ![](https://cdn.nevsky.me/content/images/2026/03/two-elements__09.png)
